Mel McKinney is a former trial attorney, lifelong outdoorsman, fly fisherman, and duck hunter. McKinney lives in Little River, California, with his wife, Susan and their black lab, Samantha. He is the author of several short stories, essays, and screenplays.

Where There’s Smoke: Thomas Dunne Books, 1999. A lovesick expatriate Cuban, an unscrupulous Southern capitalist, and a sanitized Mafia heir scratching a mid-life itch come together in this barbed tale centered around a cache of Cuban cigars secured for JFK just before he tightened the embargo in 1963.
